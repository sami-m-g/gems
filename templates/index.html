Introduction

The Greater Cairo Metro Line 4 is a new project that will link 6th of October city with the capital & New Cairo as well. The line is implemented over 2 phases, with overall distance 42 km including 38 stations. The line is expected to transport about 2 million passengers per day. Our Project studies the processes of different stages of the Grand Egyptian Museum station (GEM St.) life cycle until implementation.
